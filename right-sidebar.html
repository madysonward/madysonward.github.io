My Life Story

...and the vision I have for my tomorrow.

Everyone has their own story to tell. Who they are, where they came from, where they're heading, where they want to be...

So this is mine.

I have had a rather interesting life thus far. I had a plethora of health issues come up in my first twenty-one years alive, and I am very thankful for them. I have spent much of my life hiding away from the harsh realities of the world, allowing myself to spiral downward into my own depression. When I was twenty-one, however, I was diagnosed with a complex (but thankfully, benign) brain tumor. It was an incredibly puzzling case for doctors at various hospitals; no two had the exact same opinion on it. However, with the help of time and medication, it is under control and not a contributing factor in my health today. That tumor gave a new perspective on life - it changed the way I view the world, instilled courage within me in tackling lesser problems, and gave me inspiration to impact our planet for the better. Not a day goes by where I am not thankful.

Technology played a huge role in my ability to be proactive about my problematic health. I came to learn that there is a frightening shortage of physician specialists out there, and even more so when it comes to their time. Often, getting in to a physician that I needed to see for the next step in treatment meant waiting anywhere from a month to six months. There were other resources available to me, such as online patient portals, but the entire process made me curious as to what this will be like as the world's population continues to grow. This is one big reason I am passionate about Artificial Intelligence. I believe AI holds great potential for helping the world - especially in areas that lack available support. I very much want to be a part of turning that potential into reality.
