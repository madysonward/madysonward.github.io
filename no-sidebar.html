Think Good. Be Good. Do Good.

My Key Values & Life's Slogan

I strongly believe that, as a human being, having your own personal values in which you implement into your daily life is of utmost importance. With our world's population being over seven billion, it can be difficult to stand out. Knowing yourself is incredibly important, especially when it comes to knowing what you absolutely believe in. I believe in goodness, and therefore hold myself to the highest standard in implementing my key values which surround just that.

Think Good. Be Good. Do Good.

To Think Good, is think positively. No one lives a perfect life, and there will be plenty of bad days. This does not mean, however, that those bad days cannot be enjoyed and lived to the fullest. As someone who has struggled with Clinical Depression since early childhood, I will never be able to express just how much a shift in my own mindset has helped. To say the results have been drastically life-altering would be an understatement.

To Be Good, for me, is to be good at whatever it is I am doing in life. I will absolutely give my outstanding effort in everything I do, and am. One of my greatest goals in life is to be so good at my career, I am able to impact this world for the better, on a global scale, because of it.

Last, but not least, to Do Good, is exactly what it sounds like. Do good for this world. This comes in many forms - volunteering, donations, educating myself on what I can do for others, protecting the planet and its inhabitants, etc.
