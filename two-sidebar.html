About Me

My life away from the screen

While I am not behind my computer screen, I am most often found enjoying from behind my horse's ears. I have been a competitive equestrian for sixteen years of my life so far, competing in hunter/jumpers, as well as dressage.

I have one horse, named Rhythm. I aqcuired him when he was just eight months old, and have been incredibly privileged to bring him up from the start. He is my pride and joy, and now eleven years old.

Horses aside, I am also passionate about reading, writing, running, and cheering on my hometown NFL team, the Indianapolis Colts.
