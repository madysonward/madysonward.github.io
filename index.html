Remempathy

My final project at Eleven Fifty Academy is a group effort. It is done with Angular 4 and Firebase Cloud. Remempathy is a website for pharmaceutical companies to use in reminding their employees of the positive impact they have on many lives. Often times, a lot of employees who are behind the scenes play a vital role in patient's health, but never get to directly see how. Remempathy provides a platform of patient stories to drive those employees even further in their patient-centered, day-to-day life.
